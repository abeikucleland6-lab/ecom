The four-minute V60

Every bag ships with a brew card, but here's the version we actually use in the shop each morning.

0:00

Bloom

Pour 60g water over 20g grounds. Let it swell and breathe.

0:45

First pour

Add water in slow circles up to 200g total, center to edge.

1:45

Second pour

Top up to 320g, keeping the bed level and undisturbed.

3:30

Drawdown

Water should clear the bed by around four minutes. Adjust grind next time if not.
